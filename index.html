THE PERSONAL BRANDING CANVAS

NameKrasen — Founder of "Lost In The Sauce"
GoalSofia's first Chamorro-identified restaurant
⚙️
COMPETENCE ▾
(What you can do)
I am only at the very most a Chef de Partie. I cannot manage a whole kitchen for myself nor do I have the guts to right now. However, my recipes from my family and some traditional cookbooks not available to be purchased here in Sofia will really help kick things off since I know techniques others here don't really have any idea about.
🧬
IDENTITY ▾
(Who you are)
I am a Chamorro-Bulgarian mixed kid. I was raised primarily in Guam but I have a lot of experience with my Bulgarian side of my family. My palette was always so used to strong island flavors, with things I'm sure many Bulgarians have no comprehension on. I never really liked how Bulgarian food seemed so simple to me, and me and my father seeked different ways on how to bring these traditional foods to the island type life. I have more experience working in Guam, with soon-to-be experience at Talents or elsewhere for my 2nd semester.
📋
PROFESSION ▾
(What you do & how)
I am a culinary student at HRC Academy. As a hobby I create YouTube videos, music production, animation, and vibe-coded software. Cooking would not be my strongest suit but would be the best profession for me in the long-term, which is why I want to master it.
✅
REASONS TO BELIEVE ▾
(Why you are credible)
My authenticity is unmatched. I have both sides of my family as well as that sort of experience, present and future. I have a high pursuit of the culinary field and I feel that it's destined for me. I've tried every asian restaurant here too and I could really only like two of them, one of them still being okay for the most part. I have every opportunity just waiting to be taken by me.
📍
ARENA ▾
I want to get into the Sofia food scene, especially in Graf Ignatiev. No documentation mentions it, but I often see many young people walking by and having their events in this area and have their own shops bundled in here. No real pacific island restaurants live here let alone in any of the surrounding areas, so something like this would be highly intriguing for anyone.
